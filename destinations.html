Hunza Valley

Located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region, Hunza Valley is famous for its breathtaking scenery, hospitable people, and ancient forts.

  • Gilgit-Baltistan
  • Best Time: April to October
  • Attractions: Altit Fort, Baltit Fort, Attabad Lake
Fairy Meadows

A beautiful grassland near the base of Nanga Parbat, known as the "Killer Mountain" and the 9th highest in the world.

  • Diamer District, Gilgit-Baltistan
  • Best Time: May to September
  • Attractions: Nanga Parbat view, hiking trails
Skardu

The gateway to the world's highest peaks, Skardu is a popular tourist destination for trekkers and mountaineers.

  • Gilgit-Baltistan
  • Best Time: April to October
  • Attractions: Shangrila Resort, Upper Kachura Lake
Deosai National Park

Deosai National Park is famous for being the world's second-highest alpine plain, its stunning seasonal wildflower blooms.

  • Gilgit-Baltistan
  • Best Time: March to October
  • A high-altitude park known for its plains and Sheosar Lake.
Rama Valley

Rama Valley is famous for its lush green meadows, stunning alpine scenery, and the beautiful Rama Lake, making it a popular destination for nature lovers.

  • Gilgit-Baltistan
  • Best Time: May to September
  • Famous for its lush green landscapes, alpine forests, and lakes.
Ghazir

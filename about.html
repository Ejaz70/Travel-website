Hello! I'm Ejaz Ahmad

A passionate traveler exploring the beautiful landscapes and rich culture of Gilgit baltistan.

My journey began three years ago when I took my first trip to the Hunza gilgit. The breathtaking beauty of the mountains, the warmth of the local people, and the rich cultural heritage completely captivated me. Since then, I've been traveling across the country, documenting my experiences through writing and photography.

Through this blog, I hope to share the beauty of Pakistan with the world and inspire others to explore this magnificent country. From the snow-capped peaks of the Karakoram to the golden beaches of the Arabian Sea, Pakistan offers diverse experiences for every type of traveler.

When I'm not traveling, I'm planning my next adventure or sharing travel tips with fellow enthusiasts. I believe that travel has the power to transform perspectives and create connections between people from different backgrounds.

My Travel Philosophy

  • Travel responsibly and sustainably
  • Respect local cultures and traditions
  • Support local communities
  • Leave no trace behind
